## Validation Method Used

For each case, the validation standard used in this session was:
1. identify a strong event source,
2. identify a price-chart or price-summary source,
3. record the mechanism and directional outcome,
4. log the case in a retrieval-friendly structure.

This was intentionally a pragmatic first pass, not a perfect archival standard.
